What is Plaintextpass?

Plaintextpass is the only password manager that stores your passwords in plaintext. Plaintextpass does nothing to keep your passwords secure.

Should I use Plaintextpass?

Do you have nothing to hide and nothing to fear? Are the contents of your bank account, email account, and social media accounts unimportant to you? Then Plaintextpass is the right password manager for you!

What do I need to sign up for Plaintextpass?

Plaintextpass relies on webauthn to secure your account. You must use a webauthn compatible browser such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge or Apple Safari to sign up with Plaintextpass.

Your computer may have a built-in webauthn authenticator. You may also use a U2F compatible dongle.
